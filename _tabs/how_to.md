> 1. __Browse Proposals__ -- Explore scientific proposals in our [blog]({{'/' | relative_url}})
> 2. __Read one which interests you__ -- You can only know if you want to endorse by reading
> 2. __Decide to Endorse__ -- When you agree with a concept and want to attach your name to a proposal, scroll to the post-bottom
> 3. __Sign In with ORCiD__ -- Use or create your researcher identifier, we will retrieve your id, name, job title, and employer from your public profile
> 4. __Endorse a Proposal__ -- Show your support for proposals you believe in; though only in aggregate publicly.
> 5. __Manage your endorsements__ -- View and optionally remove your proposal endorsements.
